<a id='definition__r7rs__car__description'></a>

#### Description

> ````
> (car pair)
> ````
> 
> 
> Returns the contents of the car field of `pair`.  Note that it is an
> error to take the car of the __empty list__.
> 
> ````
> (car '(a b c))          ===>  a
> (car '((a) b c d))      ===>  (a)
> (car '(1 . 2))          ===>  1
> (car '())               ===>  #error
> ````
> 
>
